Anabelle Couleau

Independent Consultant in Data Science

Hello, I'm Anabelle

My approach is interdisplinary at the intersection of Data Science, Earth Observations and Climate Change . I am currently working as a Consultant at the Inter-American Development Bank (IDB) in the Water and Sanitation Division with a focus on water access and quality, and waste related projects under extreme weather shocks.

I am skilled for most data-science steps: from data pre-processing, application of AI models, deployment of the models, to visualization.

During my doctoral studies at the University of Illinois, I started to work in the field of data science and machine learning, focusing on analyzing high-frequency data. Following my academic journey, I embarked on a role as a researcher and professor in Colombia. During this time, I spearheaded projects addressing disaster risks , particularly landslides, and enhancing climate resilience for agricultural sectors, both crucial issues in tropical regions. Driven by my deepening interest in data science and MLOPs (Machine Learning Operations), I made the transition from academia to become an independent consultant, dedicating myself to innovative projects at the intersection of climate data science and artificial intelligence .
